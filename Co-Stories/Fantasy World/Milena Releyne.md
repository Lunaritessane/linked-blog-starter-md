[[Fantasy World]]

Resides in [[Eleira]] Empire. Former princess of [[Saklos]], now queen of [[Aurel]], wife of [[Damionus Evernon]]. Mother of [[Celseis Evenon]]. Acquaintances with [[Rayner]]. Previously engaged with [[Zenah Maia]]. 

Daughter of [[Inel Releyne]] and [[Lisseis]]. Sister of [[Deris Releyne]]. Niece of [[Mischeal Releyne]], [[Mila Releyne]], and [[Asimir Releyne]].

Witnessed the downfall of Saklos during [[The Great Disaster]]. Later ran away to Aurel.
